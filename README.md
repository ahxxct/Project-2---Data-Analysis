# Project 2 - Data Analysis

## Project Title:

Analysis of Consumer Preferences Between Local and International Clothing Brands

## Description:

This project analyzes consumer preferences between local and international clothing brands using survey data. The goal is to understand the key factors that influence purchasing decisions such as price, quality, and brand perception.

## Objective:

To determine whether consumers prefer local or international brands and identify the main factors affecting their choices.

## Dataset:

The dataset contains survey responses collected from university students aged between 18 and 24. It includes information about shopping behavior, preferences, and influencing factors.

## Project Files:

* **DataSet.csv** → dataset used for analysis
* **Local_vs_Internationa.ipynb** → Jupyter Notebook with data analysis and visualization
* **Report data sense.pdf** → Final report explaining insights and conclusions

## Key Insights:

* Consumers are divided between local and international brands.
* Local brands are preferred for affordability and value for money.
* International brands are preferred for quality and trust.
* Price and quality are the most important factors in decision-making.

## Tools Used:

* Python
* Pandas
* Matplotlib
* Jupyter Notebook

## Conclusion:

The analysis shows a balance between local and international brands. While international brands are associated with higher quality and trust, local brands remain competitive due to lower prices and better value.

## Limitations:

* Small sample size (50 responses)
* Focused on young consumers (18–24 years old)
* Possible bias due to survey method
